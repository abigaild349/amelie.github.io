Amelie: The Cat

Amelie

Amelie is a five year old cat who just moved to the big apple! She was found in Burbank, California at a local animal shelter by her mom and is so happy to be in a new city! The plane ride was rough, but she has been slowly finding her footing and loves her new roommates! Follow her on all her adventures as NYC's newest resident!
